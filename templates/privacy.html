Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app (e.g., pages visited, session duration).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to provide and improve the Spendly service:

  • To display your expense history and summaries within your account.
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To improve app performance and fix issues based on usage patterns.

We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Your expense data is only accessible to you through your authenticated account. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use third-party infrastructure providers (e.g., hosting) to operate the service, but these providers do not have access to your financial data and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the app.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the app. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
